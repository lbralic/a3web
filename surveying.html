Surveying

Surveying is the science and art of determining the relative position of the point above, on, or beneath the earths surface and location points in the field. It is the cornerstone of Geomatics and is the computerization and digitization of data collection, processing, analysis, and output. Here at GeomSolutions, we have surveying technicians and analysts that are highly trained in the collection of data in the field and experts in computerization and digitization of the collected data. Our team can help to collect and produce surveying data for a variety of different surveying fields such as; topographic, hydrographic, route, property, aerial, and construction.
