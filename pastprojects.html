Past Projects

When we first started our company our most common project was completing parcel surveys. Although we have taken on larger scale projects in the last several years, we still routinely take on surveys whenever we can. Pictured here is a sample of a survey we conducted on a property located along the Scugog River. We conducted this survey after a client bought the property and wanted to determine exactly where the boundaries were located prior to further developing their property.

This project was completed for Jeff Crow of Jeff Crow Tourism to illustrate land use in Newfoundland. The purpose of the project was to highlight some of the natural landscape surrounding St. John's, NL, as a bid to increase tourism. This was part of a joint project between Jeff Crow Tourism, based in Lindsay, and Jane Summers Tourism, based in St. John's, to promote the tourism bundle pack that takes you on a journey from Fleming, ON all the way to St. John's NL. The map was a printed large scale map that was displayed in both parties offices during the tourist season.

The population density map for Vancouver, British Columbia, was created by our fantastic cartography team for Sanders Solutions. Sanders Solutions is one of Lindsay's leading construction companies that develops environmentally conscientious apartment buildings across Canada, where each complex is fitted with leading edge innovative clean energy solutions. The purpose of this map was to help Sanders Solutions narrow down potential building sites based on population density and the proximity to greenspace. Based on our analysis, Sanders Solutions has built a highly lucrative and beautiful apartment complex in an idyllic location.

One of our larger, ongoing projects is the classification on land surrounding Lindsay and Peterborough, Ontario for the municipal government. The purpose of this project is to map out the agricultural fields and wetlands in the area for conservation and agricultural funding purposes. As the project is ongoing, additional details are not available for the public.
