Meet Our Team

I am a Perth based GIS consultant specializing in spatial analysis and programming. Prior to starting at GeomSolutions I completed my MSc at the Memorial University where my research focused on modelling forest restoration scenarios after moose overbrowsing. Much of my research utilized aerial imagery analysis and model building which led me to develop a passion for GIS. I have now been working with GeomSolutions for five years where I have worked on a variety of projects including capturing drone imagery of conservation land, building spatial models to analyze deforestation trends, and using LANDSAT imagery to classify land use. GeomSolutions has a fantastic team, and we look forward to working with you on your next project!

Lucija Bralic is a GIS Analyst at GeomSolutions. Lucija began working at GeomSolutions in June 2020 as a GIS Technician. The following June, she joined the office team and has played a vital role in various projects, which include wildlife monitoring, risk assessments, and ecological restoration projects. With a background in Environmental Biology, Lucija leverages her scientific knowledge and GIS experience to provide the best possible services for GeomSolutions’ clients.
